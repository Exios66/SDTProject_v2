Privacy Policy

At Dark Triad Personality Assessments, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information you provide, such as your name and email address, when you register for an account or contact us.
  • Assessment responses and results when you complete our personality assessments.
  • Usage data, such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited on our site, collected through cookies and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services, including generating personality assessment results.
  • Communicate with you about your account, assessments, and updates to our services.
  • Analyze usage trends and preferences to enhance user experience and develop new features.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and the rights of others.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this Privacy Policy. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • If required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process.
  • When we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may update or delete your account information at any time. You may also opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in those emails.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of the policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@darktriadassessments.com.
